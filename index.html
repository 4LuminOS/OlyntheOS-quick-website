Lumin is a LLM, he uses advanced natural language processing to understand your queries and provide intelligent responses. It can help with coding, writing, research, actions across your LuminOS and much more... while also keeping your data private and secure on your lovely machine.

He lives at the center of LuminOS. The AI model is optimized for performance and runs efficiently on modern hardware, providing quick responses without requiring cloud connectivity.

Tech & Requirements

We will give more technical information on LuminOS very soon! Thank you.

Minimum Requirements (To run LuminOS + Lumin)

  • Processor: 6 Cores or higher (Must support AVX2 instructions)
  • RAM: 8 GB+ (The AI model requires ~5GB of free RAM to load).
  • Storage: 64GB+ free space (use of an SSD highly recommended).
  • GPU/Graphics: NVIDIA/AMD Dedicated GPU (Accelerates Lumin's response time); integrated graphics will also work.

Recommended Specifications

  • Processor: 8+ Cores for optimal AI performance
  • RAM: 16 GB+ for smoother multitasking
  • Storage: 128GB+ NVMe SSD for best experience
  • GPU: Dedicated GPU with 4GB+ VRAM for fastest AI responses
