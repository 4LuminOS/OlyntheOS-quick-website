Features

01

Powered by Lumin

An integrated AI assistant based on Llama 3. Runs 100% locally — no cloud, no data leaks, no subscription. It helps you write, navigate, think, and work efficiently.

Lumin uses advanced natural language processing to understand your queries and provide intelligent responses. It helps with coding, writing, research, and actions across OlyntheOS — while keeping your data private and secure on your machine.

The AI model is optimized for performance and runs efficiently on modern hardware, providing quick responses without cloud connectivity.

02

Privacy First

Zero telemetry by default. Your data never leaves local storage. No one scrutinizes it. You are the sole owner — Swiss philosophy applied to computing.

OlyntheOS has no tracking, no analytics, and no hidden data collection. Your files, conversations with Lumin, and usage patterns stay completely private.

Built with privacy-focused tools and open-source components you can audit yourself.

03

Debian Core

Built on the rock-solid foundation of Debian 13 "Trixie". Stability, modern software compatibility, and AI-driven actions — all in one.

Debian provides a stable, secure, and well-tested foundation. Thousands of packages, excellent hardware support, and the reliability of enterprise Linux with modern desktop flexibility.

Regular security updates and long-term support keep your system functional and safe.

04

Batteries Included

Productivity out of the box: OnlyOffice, VLC, Timeshift, and full Flatpak support. Everything you need from day one.

Pre-installed applications cover a full office suite, media player, and system backup tool — plus access to thousands of Flatpak applications.

Software management works with both graphical tools and the command line.

Requirements

Minimum

To run OlyntheOS + Lumin AI

  • CPU 6 cores or higher (AVX2 required)
  • RAM 8 GB+ (Lumin requires ~5 GB)
  • Storage 64 GB free — SSD strongly recommended
  • GPU Integrated graphics work; dedicated GPU accelerates AI response time

Recommended

For the best experience

  • CPU 8+ cores
  • RAM 16 GB+
  • Storage 128 GB+ NVMe SSD
  • GPU Dedicated GPU with 4 GB+ VRAM

Compare

OlyntheOS Alpha

  • Mostly stable, thanks to Debian 13
  • LTS foundation, yet still up-to-date
  • Great selection of FOSS out of the box
  • Hard to run on older hardware
  • Experimental — not ready for daily use yet

General GNU/Linux

  • Generally well-established
  • Long-term and non-LTS options available
  • Thorough documentation, many contributors
  • Lacks meaningful AI-driven features
  • UI often feels dated or inconsistent

Based on general, well-known distributions. Does not apply to all cases.
